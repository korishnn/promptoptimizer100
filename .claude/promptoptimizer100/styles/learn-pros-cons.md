# learn-pros-cons

Analyze the pros and cons of the described approach or tool:
- Structure evaluation across multiple dimensions: performance, maintainability, learning curve, ecosystem, scalability, cost
- Present each pro and con with specific evidence, not general claims
- Include a summary recommendation with a confidence level (high/medium/low)
- Compare against alternatives where relevant
- Acknowledge context: pros and cons may shift depending on team size, scale, or use case
- Highlight dealbreakers that would disqualify the option entirely
- List tradeoffs where a pro in one dimension is a con in another
